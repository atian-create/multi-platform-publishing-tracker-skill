# Sample Publishing Checklist

## Publishing Scope

一个母内容包，要拆成小红书图文、公众号长文、朋友圈短文、X thread、短视频脚本和 7 天复盘。

## Platform / Account Matrix

| Platform | Account | Required Version | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Xiaohongshu | main creator account | graphic note + cover | ready | needs final keywords |
| WeChat public account | main account | long article | published | review after 48h |
| WeChat Moments | personal account | short copy | draft | shorten opening |
| X | build-in-public account | thread | idea | extract 5 points |
| Douyin | short video account | 60s script | blocked | needs video hook |
| Bilibili | creator account | title + description | skipped | not worth doing this time |

## Content Rows

| Content Asset | Target Platforms | Current Status | Missing Item | Review Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Main article | WeChat | published | none | 48h |

| Short copy | Moments | draft | opening line | same day |
| Thread | X | idea | 5-point outline | 24h |
| Short video | Douyin | blocked | hook and script | after script exists |

## Missing Items

- Xiaohongshu still needs 8-12 publishing keywords.
- Moments copy needs a softer first sentence.
- X thread needs a compact 5-point structure.
- Douyin should not be published until the first 3-second hook is clear.
